## Final Review

### Before Clicking Submit

1. **Read everything one more time**
   - Check for typos
   - Verify all links work
   - Ensure images display correctly

2. **Test the user journey**
   - Can someone clone and run your project?
   - Is the demo video compelling?
   - Do screenshots tell a clear story?

3. **Verify requirements**
   - Submitted to correct hackathon
   - All required fields filled
   - Meets all guidelines

4. **Get feedback**
   - Have someone review your submission
   - Test setup instructions with fresh eyes
   - Watch demo video as if you're a judge

### Submission Timing

- [ ] Submit at least 1 hour before deadline
- [ ] Keep backup of all materials
